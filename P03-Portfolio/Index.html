about me

Hi, I'm Jules, a student of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University. I love to design stories into everything I create, from point-and-click narratives and VR games to installation art. This probably comes from my passion for excellent fiction, and my upbringing in a house full of geeks. Seriously, don't get me started on Harry Potter or The Lord of the Rings.
